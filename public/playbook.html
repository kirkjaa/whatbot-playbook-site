Company: [Company name]

Turbo Consulting Prompt 
Run a Venture Consultant review in turbo mode.

Context:
[Summarize company, product, traction, business model, market, funding goal]

Ask all five specialists to analyze:
1. Market size, growth, and timing
2. Business model and pricing
3. Competitive positioning and moat
4. Financial projections, unit economics, runway
5. Pitch narrative, investor objections, and funding readiness

Skeptical Investor Stress Test 
Act as a skeptical Series A investor.

Challenge the latest venture analysis:
- What would make you pass immediately?
- Which assumptions need evidence this month?
- Which metrics are missing or weak?
- What is the clearest path to a fundable milestone?

Be direct, specific, and quantify the risk where possible.
